Space Scout

A Space Exploration Company

Short Elevator Pitch


Our company wishes to bring the stars within reach, and to research the infinity of space.

Long Elevator Pitch


Space Scount wishes to pioneer in space exploration and space exploration technology. Research into Cryogenics, Faster Than Light Travel, and other space technologies are among our top concerns. We believe we have much to learn from other celestial bodies and any life we may find on them. We also wish to help in finding new planets our race may colonize in order to make sure humanity lives on and can break free of the confines that our earths limited resources give us.

Personas

  • Donators: People looking to donate to the cause of space exploration.
  • Astronaughts: People looking to pilot space fairing vessels.
